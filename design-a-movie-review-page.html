Interstellar is a critically acclaimed 2014 epic science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan that explores themes of survival, love, and the boundaries of space and time. The movie follows a team of astronauts who travel through a wormhole to find a new habitable planet for humanity as Earth faces extinction due to environmental disaster.
